20 July 2026: Today, Matrix Cola proudly unveils its new global visual identity system, introducing a bold, modern, and scalable brand look designed to strengthen recognition across every consumer touchpoint.

Built around the strategic vision of making every Matrix Cola experience instantly recognizable, the refreshed identity marks a new chapter in the brand’s evolution, reinforcing consistency while enhancing its presence across regional and international markets.

The new visual system highlights Matrix Cola’s signature brand assets, including its vibrant color palette, dynamic graphic elements, bold typography, and iconic “X” design that defines the brand across cans, bottles, retail environments, marketing materials, and digital platforms.

“Matrix Cola continues to evolve with the needs of today’s consumers while staying true to the brand they know and trust,” said [CEO / Marketing Director Name], Matrix Cola. “This visual refresh strengthens our identity, creates a more consistent brand experience, and builds a stronger foundation for future growth across every market we serve.”

As part of the refresh, the Matrix Cola Zero Sugar range also receives an updated design featuring a more distinctive visual language, including larger “Zero Sugar” typography, bold black graphic accents, and premium packaging details that clearly differentiate it while maintaining a strong connection to the Matrix Cola master brand.

The refreshed identity will gradually roll out across Jordan, the Middle East, and key international markets, creating a unified and impactful brand experience for consumers wherever they encounter Matrix Cola.
